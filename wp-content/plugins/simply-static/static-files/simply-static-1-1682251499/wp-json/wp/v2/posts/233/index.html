\n<\/a>Hi Everyone. Today I am going to tell you one trick that you haven\u2019t know. Have you ever heard that you can display your name in taskbar at bottom?
\nAs everybody know there is a clock, in bottom right hand side of your taskbar. Today I my trick I will tell you \u201cHow can you display your name at the position of AM and PM of clock.\u201d So just follow the procedure.
\n
\n
\n<\/a>
\n1. Click on that time block at bottom right corner. Then click on \u201cChange time and date settings\u201d.
\n2. Then in Date and Time settings click on \u201cChange date and time..\u201d <\/p>\n


\n
\n<\/a>
\n3. Then in next window click on link \u201cChange calender settings\u201d.
\n4. Then in customize format window. Switch tab to \u201cTime\u201d at top.
\n5. And their change AM and PM symbol to the text that you want to display.
\n
\n
\n<\/a>
\n6. In my case I have set it to name \u201cShreyas\u201d and \u201cMike\u201d. After that click on apply and now you can see your name (or text that you have set) instead of AM and PM.
\n7. You can reset this settings by clicking reset on same time settings.
